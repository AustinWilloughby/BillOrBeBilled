# Billy Bot
- Bill or be Billed
- Keyboard & Mouse
- Black & White
## Adventure
- Exploration
  The player will run around the world and pick up items to bring back to their storage warehouse.
- Puzzle
  The player will have to fit tetris shaped items into their box shaped storage unit.
- Dialogue
  The player will get tickets requesting items. The player will be rewarded with money. The player will have to search through their warehouse to find the item(s) requested in the ticket.
- Inventory
  The player will have to fit items they pick up out in the world into their limited storage unit. When they bring items back to their warehouse, they will place them into a large storage pile.
